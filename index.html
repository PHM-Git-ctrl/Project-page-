Coffee cupcake with whipped cream

The perfect cake to accompany your everyday coffee.
It's very easy and on top of that it looks wonderful, it will definitely impress.
Do it and then tell me what you think!

Time 1h10min
Yield: 12 servings
Difficulty: easy

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cup (tea) of warm water
  • 1 tablespoon of instant coffee
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of oil
  • 2cups (tea) of sugar
  • 2/3 cup (tea) of cocoa powder
  • 2 and 1/2 cups (tea) of wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 2 cups (tea) of ready whipped cream
  • Coffee beans to decorate
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of instant coffee
  • 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Preparation mode

In a blender, beat the water, instant coffee, eggs, oil, sugar and chocolate powder until smooth.
Transfer to a bowl, add the flour and yeast and mix with a spoon.
Pour into cupcake liners lined with paper liners and place on a large baking tray, one next to the other.
Bake in a preheated medium oven for 25 minutes or until cooked and golden.
Remove and let cool.

For the filling, heat all the ingredients over medium heat, stirring well until thickened.
Turn off the heat, let it cool and place in a piping bag with a pearl tip. Set aside.
Place the whipped cream in a piping bag with a star tip and set aside.

To assemble, make a hole in the center of the cupcakes and fill with the reserved coffee brigadeiro.
Decorate the surfaces of the cupcakes with the reserved whipped cream, making circles.
Decorate with coffee beans and refrigerate until ready to serve.
